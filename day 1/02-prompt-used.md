# Prompt Used

You are an expert research analyst. Summarize the following academic paper in 150-200 words. Your summary should include: 

The paper's main purpose and scope

The key methodologies or approaches discussed

The major findings and conclusions

The key challenges and open problems identified

Use clear, concise language. Preserve factual accuracy and do not invent information not present in the text. Prioritize the most important insights over minor details.
